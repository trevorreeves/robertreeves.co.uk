Parking There

There is a car park at the venue (17 spaces), when this is full parking will be on the surrounding residential streets.

Staying There

The venue is only approx. 15 minutes drive from the city centre, so staying anywhere in and around the centre would be suitable and there are plenty of options. The website TripAdvisor is a good start to find accommodation if you need a suggestion.
