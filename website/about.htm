More About Robert

Robert is an officially accredited Rolex repairer, with a workshop acredited by Rolex.

Robert has been a Member of the British Horological Institue since 1977.

Robert has been repairing watches for private customers and jewellers in the UK since 1975.

Robert has trained at the Omega Watch Company in London, ETA Watch Factory in Switzerland, Rolex Watch Company UK & the British Horological Institute Education Trust.

Robert currently repairs watches for xx jewellers across the UK.

Robert is an active member of the BHI South West Branch and frequently gives presentations on watch repairing best practices.

Robert has taught BHI watch repair courses at St. Loye's College, Exeter.
